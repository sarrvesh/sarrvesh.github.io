Welcome to Sarrvesh’s website¶

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Max Planck Institute for Radio Astronomy working with the MeerKAT radio telescope.

Previously, I worked at ASTRON as a Telescope Scientist working with the LOw Frequency ARray (LOFAR). For my PhD, I studied the non-thermal emission and magnetic fields in nearby galaxies using the LOFAR and the Westerbork radio telescopes. You can find the electronic copy of my thesis here.

You can download my CV here. You can access my list of publication on NASA ADS.
