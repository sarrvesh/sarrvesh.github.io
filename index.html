Welcome to Sarrvesh’s website¶

I am a telescope scientist working with the LOw Frequency ARray (LOFAR) at ASTRON. For my PhD, I studied the non-thermal emission and magnetic fields in nearby galaxies using the LOFAR and the Westerbork radio telescopes. You can find the electronic copy of my thesis here.
