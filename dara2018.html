Development in Africa with Radio Astronomy (DARA) - Mozambique 2018¶

Overview¶

Later this year, in June, Prof. Dalmiro Maia (University of Porto) and I will be teaching radio astronomy at a workshop in Maputo, Mozambique. The workshop is organized as part of the DARA project.

The workshop will be held at Eduardo Mondlane University from June 18, 2018 to June 29, 2018.
